Decision ID: D-006

Date: 2026-04-16

Decision Title: Replace buffered 1.0 V reference with trimmed 0.9 V reference path

Status: active

Approved In Meeting: M-012

Decision Summary:
Revised D-003 after radiation drift simulations and hot-corner headroom analysis showed the
buffered 1.0 V ADC Reference Voltage path would not hold settling margin after total dose.

Decision Impact:
- Analog team inserted trim range expansion and removed one gain stage from the reference buffer.
- Verification updated mixed-signal regressions for new trim tables.
