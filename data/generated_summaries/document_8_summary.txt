**Project Context**

This document outlines the architecture selection board's decision to adopt a segmented capacitor DAC architecture, approved as D-002. The board selected the segmented capacitor DAC architecture, and the bootstrapped front-end switch was approved after comparing energy, linearity, and Radiation Hardening margin against charge-redistribution alternatives. The decision was made based on the assessment of the ADC Reference Voltage drift and Lithography Alignment sensitivity, with mitigation strategies in place for radiation and hot corner ownership. The board's prioritization of this architecture was based on its ability to meet the budget constraints and maintain the integrity of the system.
