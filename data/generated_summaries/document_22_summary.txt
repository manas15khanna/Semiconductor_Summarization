**Meeting Summary:**

This meeting focused on evaluating the lithography alignment exposure of the layout and lithography alignment sensor array.

**Key Findings:**

*   The array edge condition caused tension between matching and density requirements.
*   The alignment sensitivity was high near the capacitor array edge, potentially necessitating dedicated overlay monitors.
*   The board agreed on the need for a dedicated overlay monitor for Lithography Alignment sensitivity, suggesting the use of radiation hardening and packaging revision.
*   The board's decision to not make new formal decisions was made, prioritizing the material that would become D-005.
