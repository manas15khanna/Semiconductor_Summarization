This document summarizes the architecture review process for the Project Context. It covers key findings, decisions, risks, and action items. The review focused on the power feasibility study (AI-001) and the architecture selection (DEC-005) for the 4-channel time-interleaved SAR ADC architecture. The decision was to select the 4-channel time-interleaved SAR ADC architecture as the program baseline, with a 430 mW power target set to 450 mW in the reference voltage generator and clock distribution. The review also addressed the radiation hardening strategy, identifying the per-block rad-hard strategy and the need for dedicated calibration. The meeting objectives were to review the SAR core schematic, reference voltage generator, clock distribution, DMC, and I/O pads, and to confirm the power budget allocation. The meeting concluded with a summary of the architectural review and a final decision.
