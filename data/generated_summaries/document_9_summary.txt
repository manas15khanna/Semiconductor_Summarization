This document summarizes the architecture review meeting held on February 25, 2025, for the review of the SAR core schematic and design of the proposed power feasibility study. The meeting focused on the power budget allocation for the 4-channel time-interleaved SAR ADC architecture and the EGR-3 layout compliance status. The decision to proceed with the 4-channel time-interleaved SAR ADC architecture was made based on the power budget constraints and the need for a margin in the power target. The review was completed by the attendees, Col. V. K. Tiwari (DRDO), PDMS, and the entire organization. The meeting concluded at 13:00 hrs with the minutes of the meeting being circulated to all attendees.
