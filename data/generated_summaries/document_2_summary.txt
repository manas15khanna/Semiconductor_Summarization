This document summarizes the meeting held on June 17, 2025, to review the status of the verification and tapeout readiness of the DEC-014 and DEC-015 TMR voter implementations.

**Key Findings:**

*   **TPMR Voter Implementation:** The DEC-014 and DEC-015 TMR voter implementations were successfully implemented and re-verified.
*   **Verification Closure:** All 22 properties proven in the verification system were successfully closed.
*   **Tapeout Readiness:** The tapeout readiness checklist was completed, and all remaining open items were identified and resolved.
*   **DRDO's Role:** Dr. Sharma is responsible for the implementation and re-verification of the DEC-014 and DEC-015 TMR voter implementations.
*   **CAP Failure:** One of the two failing TMR voter implementations (DEC-014) was identified as a blocking issue, which was addressed by the DEC-014 implementation.

**Risks:**

*   **Tapeout Schedule Slip:** The DEC-014 implementation was delayed by two weeks due to a potential issue with the input pipeline register and the comparator regeneration path transistors.
*   **Gate-Level Simulation:** The DRC/LVS simulation was delayed due to the extended runtime of the DCF run.
*   **ENOB Failure:** The ENOB failure was identified at the worst-case radiation corner, which is a critical area for the solution.
*   **TMR Simultaneous Dual-Bit Upset:** The TMR simultaneous dual-bit upset vulnerability was identified, which is a significant issue.
