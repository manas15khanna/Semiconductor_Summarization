Decision ID: D-001

Date: 2026-01-22

Decision Title: Freeze mission-level ADC requirements

Status: active

Approved In Meeting: M-002

Decision Summary:
Approved the baseline requirement set for a 12-bit, 5 MSPS radiation-hardened SAR ADC with
guaranteed operation through 100 krad total ionizing dose and single-event upset recovery within
two conversion cycles.

Decision Impact:
- Requirements document locked for architecture trade study.
- Verification team aligned coverage model to the 12-bit transfer function and SEE recovery goals.
